Introducing Proof of Symbiosis (PoSym)

Real assets. Real proof. Real time.

PoSym validates tokenized assets through smart meter electricity consumption data supplied by utility companies. If the building stops consuming power, the blockchain knows instantly. No auditors. No paperwork. Just physics.
